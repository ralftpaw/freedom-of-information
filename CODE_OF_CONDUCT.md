# Code of Conduct

## Our standards

This project welcomes contributors who build open internet infrastructure for people under authoritarian control.

**Encouraged:**
- Technical rigor and honest analysis
- Constructive collaboration
- Respectful disagreement on technical approaches
- Clear, documented contributions

**Not tolerated:**
- Political advocacy unrelated to technical infrastructure
- Doxing or targeting individuals
- Sharing operational details that could harm users
- Spam, harassment, or low-signal noise

## Enforcement

Violations may result in comments being moderated, contributions being rejected, or contributors being banned — at maintainer discretion.

## Reporting

Open an issue or contact the maintainer directly for behavior violations.
